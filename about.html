About Us

Building Better, Sustainable Homes

North South Building focuses on creating homes that are not only beautiful and functional but also good for the environment. Since starting in 2015 in Clifton Grove, we've been committed to building high-quality, energy-efficient homes that meet the needs of today without compromising tomorrow.

“Creating energy-efficient homes with a heart for sustainability is not just our business; it's our passion and promise to you.”
–Jaime Kelso

Our Mission

To build homes that fulfil your dreams and protect the future, focusing on energy-efficiency and superior design for sustainable living.

Our vision

Leading with skill and innovation, we craft homes that embody sustainability and traditional finesse, shaping greener communities for tomorrow.

Our Values

Upholding excellence and honesty in every build, and designing energy-smart homes that respect both our clients' desires and the planet's needs.

Our Services

New Home Construction

As your trusted new home builders in the Orange region, we construct custom, architect-designed homes with a strong emphasis on sustainability and energy efficiency. Our homes are designed to maximise solar gain, provide superior insulation, and minimise air leakage, ensuring that they are not only beautiful but also cost-effective and environmentally friendly.


Home Extensions

Expand your living space with our expertly designed extensions. Each extension is crafted to enhance your home's functionality while maintaining energy efficiency. Our house extension builders integrate the latest in sustainable building practices to ensure your new space is both beautiful and environmentally conscious.


Home Renovations

Upgrade and enhance your existing home with our bespoke renovation services. We focus on improving insulation, sealing, and overall energy efficiency. As experienced home renovation contractors, we focus on improving insulation, sealing, and overall energy efficiency. Our renovations breathe new life into old spaces, making them more comfortable, sustainable, and tailored to your needs.
